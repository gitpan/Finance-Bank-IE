Your Account Balance

Your Account Balance is the most up to date balance available. It is the balance at banking close of business on the previous banking day adjusted by any transactions, which have been presented up to this time. This balance is updated during the day as transactions are presented and the status of these items will not be confirmed until the next banking close of business. This balance is subject to the clearance of any uncleared cheques lodged recently.

Your Account Balance

Your Account Balance reflects the position at close of business on the previous working day, subject to any transactions that have not yet been applied to your account.
